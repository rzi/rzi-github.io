Introduction

This application was createt base of needs to eliminate paper and optimisation managment of lunch in company. Before application, every orders was written on paper and after ordering process rewrite to excel. This process was sensitive for mistake on several steps of ordering process and also on monthly invoice settlement. This application was creater togeter with camtronic.pl developer

elunch.ct8.pl

Client side functionality

Every workers in company can order lunch. First of all worker should by registered .This process will be described on administration module, at this momet imprtant is that each waker after registratiion have his/her id what we called sesa number and password.

Aferl login you are move to lunch welcome page. You have two possibility, first to order lunch, second to check history of orders

Let's go and order your lunch. You can do it until 9.00am for today and also for next days. We have two different lunch supplier and you can swetch between them clicking tab Mucha (name of first supplier) or Tramp (name of second supplier. If you would like to order your lunch for next day click on calendar.

Order your dish from menu cliking on icon fork and knife.

On top table, after order you will see what exacty you ordered and what is dish price and deduction (discount, what company paid)

If you ordered wrong dish, you can delete it clicking bin icon
